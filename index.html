Planning Oman? Undoubtedly a great choice to spend your holidays or meet up with your family and friends and make every moment count. What Is The Oman evisa?

A soft copy of a legal document that grants the eligible foreign visitors to come and stay on its land for a fixed number of days is the Oman eVisa. To apply, one must fill in an Application Form and meet all the eligibility criteria set by the government and the immigration department. Once the application is submitted, the details will go through strict checking and verification and once complete, your visa application will be approved and your Visa will be sent to you.

Oman Visas Types That You Can Apply Online

There are a few types of Oman e Visa available for anyone to apply, you can check the Oman tourist visa that suits you the best and proceed with getting your eVisa.

10 Days Single Entry Visa - Perfect choice without a second thought in mind for those looking forward to a short trip to Oman for tourism or visiting purposes. This Visa grants a stay of 10 days with a single entry and is valid for 90 days from the date of issuance. 

30 Days Single Entry Visa - Valid for 90 days, this Visa will allow you to stay a little longer in Oman that is for a time period of 30 Days in the country. Best for those who are visiting Oman for meeting family or friends but at the same time, this is also a better choice for travelers that are in search of more time to explore Oman and stay for a longer period of time.

Who Needs A Visa For Oman?

When it comes to traveling internationally, everyone will need a Visa in order to be granted a stay on foreign lands. Citizens of all eligible countries will require a valid Visa to enter and stay in Oman and the duration of stay will be strictly followed as per the visa that one has applied for. 

The citizens of the GCC countries are not required to Apply Oman visa as they can freely enter this Nation without any issues or Visas required.

Tip : Apply for a Visa through us and get it delivered before the deadline. We work directly with the immigration department to minimize processing time and we have an approval rate of 99.9% which is the highest in this industry.

Oman Visa Application Requirements 

To be eligible, you will need to fill the criteria below.

  • Your Passport Must Be Issued By An Eligible Country.
  • Your Passport Must Be Valid For At-least 6 Months.
  • You Must Submit A Passport-Size Photo On A Plain Background.
  • Your Personal Details Such As Your Name And Date Of Birth Will Be Needed.
  • A Valid Email Address Which Will Be Used To Receive Your Visa Is Required.
  • Must Have A Clean Track Of Criminal Records.
  • Enough Funds To Support You Throughout The Trip.

If you have these Oman visa requirements fulfilled, you can easily apply for an eVisa.

Do Transit And Ship Passengers Need An Oman Visa?

If the layover between flights and the time needed for the ships to pass by is less than 6 hours, an in-transit visa is not required and if the layover is within 24 hours but more than the 6 hours threshold, a visa on arrival will be granted to all the passengers for not more than 24 hours. If you wish to explore Oman in between your flights, you can apply for an in-transit Visa.

Why Travel to Oman With an eVisa?

Travel to Oman With an e-Visa

When to Apply: Oman Visa Processing Time

Normally, visa application may take time so it is always better to apply beforehand to avoid any inconvenience at the time of travel. However, if the need of visa arises urgently, one can easily apply for an urgent eVisa for Oman with us.

What to Do With An eVisa In Oman

Offering the same authenticity as the physical Visa, with an eVisa, you can explore the nation of Oman freely or pay a visit to your friends and family. Additional information on the Oman eVisa

To better understand, here are some additional things to understand the Online Oman Visa better.

  • In case of loss, theft or expiry, you will need to apply for a new Visa.
